About Us

We are a group of first year B Tech Students, who propose to use technology to find solutions for everyday problems of VITians and society as a whole. Commute is a part of everyday life at any college, including VIT. Reporting to lectures on time is crucial for the students as well as faculty to ensure smooth learning. We have observed that shuttles become extremely vital in commute, especially when weather is unfavorable, we are not in our best of health, or when lectures are back-to-back across distant blocks.
In such situations, it is important to know the whereabouts of the shuttle to make best use of them. We propose a website and an app to follow, to acquaint the users with the shuttle timings and enable them to find nearest shuttle.
